    inline direction - left to right, or direction of a line of written text (L-R in English)
    block direction - up and down, or cross axis of the direction of a line of written text

    content box
	• size of content + padding
	• size it using width or height, or inline-size (size of the inline box) or block-size (size of the block box)

    alternative box model (box-sizing: border-box)

    background styling
    background-image
    small images tile automatically
    background-repeat: no-repeat, repeat-x, repeat-y (along y axis), repeat (in both dirs)
    background-size
    takes length or % vals, or cover (img just large enough to cover the box area while keeping aspect ratio - likely to still "crop" the image), or contain (browser will make img right size to fit box, may end up with gaps on top/bottom due to img's aspect ratio)
    background-position
    default (0,0) - left top
    horizontal, vertial
    e.g. top center, 20px 10%, top 20px right 10px
    ** is it better to use background-img or <img> to add an image?
    ***screen readers cannot parse background images, so they should be purely decoration (important content should be part of the HTML page)
    background attachment: scroll, fixed, local
    local - fixes the bg to the element it is set on, so when the element scrolls the bg scrolls
    scroll -fixed bg to page, not element
    fixed - fixes bg to element, content or page scrolling does nothing
