who are we?

we are a group who love to eat, who enjoy exploring the origins and processes that go into our food, and who cherish the opportunities we have to enjoy something delicious

what do we do?

once per month :
    our volunteer members host the club for members-only dinner parties with a handcrafted drinks menu from our partner, the lounge while a handselected team of volunteers and the host work together to craft a menu that caters towards a special theme and utilizes fresh and seasonal goods from our list of local partners below. dessert commonly features a special showstopper from our longtime friend and partner, le boulangerie, a bespoke bakery run by two lovely ladies who churn out amazingly seasoning and visual masterpieces


    to keep our costs maintainable while also not unduly placing a burden on the chosen host, each seat costs between $10 and $30 depending on the menu and location



to sign up to host, reach out to coord@getbaked.com. to sign up for our seatlist, reach out to eat@getbaked.com

special events

twice per year, our president and founder loves to take us all somewhere special. each club member will be invited to an exclusive club-only trip for one week to a variety of destinations. previously, the club have gone...


 - napa valley wine making source and wine tasting tour

 - private estate in the hamptons with a visiting private chef each night for a custom cooking course

 - chartered private yacht cruise and specialty seafood course


events, travel, and accomodation are paid for by your annual club dues along with the generosity of our president and club partners
